# First Day

On December 12, 2025, Winnipeg Transit 295 operated on the 1-24 run of route **BLUE**, completing one round-trip between U of M and Unicity Hub.
This marked the first day of operation for the New Flyer XHE60 in Canada.

## XHE60

The XHE60 is a 60-foot (articulated) low-floor hydrogen fuel cell electric bus manufactured by New Flyer. The model number stands for the following:

- X: Xcelsior (product line)
- H: Hydrogen fuel cell
- E: Electric
- 60: 60-foot length
